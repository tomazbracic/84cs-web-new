Privacy Policy

Coffee and Water - Hydrate App

Last updated: August 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Hydrate ("we," "us," or "our") handles your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). Hydrate is a simple coffee and water intake tracking app developed by 84-CS.

The short version: We don't collect any of your data. Everything stays on your device.

2. No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information. Hydrate operates entirely offline and locally on your device. Specifically:

  • No user registration or account creation is required
  • No personal information is requested or collected
  • No usage data or analytics are collected
  • No device information is tracked
  • No network requests are made to external servers
  • No cookies or tracking technologies are used

3. Local Data Storage

All data within Hydrate is stored locally on your device:

  • Your daily coffee and water intake records
  • Your personal hydration goals and preferences
  • App settings and customizations

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties. If you uninstall the app, all data is permanently deleted from your device.

4. No Third-Party Services

Hydrate does not integrate with or use any third-party services, analytics tools, advertising networks, or cloud storage providers. The app functions completely independently on your device.

5. No Data Sharing

Since we don't collect any data, we have nothing to share, sell, or disclose to third parties. Your hydration tracking data remains completely private to you.

6. Children's Privacy

Hydrate is suitable for users of all ages. Since we don't collect any personal information from anyone, including children, there are no special privacy concerns for younger users. The app can be safely used by anyone who wants to track their daily coffee and water intake.

7. App Distribution

Hydrate is distributed through official app stores:

  • Apple App Store (iOS)
  • Google Play Store (Android) - coming soon

Please note that these app stores may collect their own analytics and usage data according to their privacy policies, but this is separate from our app and not controlled by us.

8. Future Changes

Currently, Hydrate is a completely offline, privacy-focused app. If we decide to add features in the future that require data collection (such as cloud sync, social features, or analytics), we will:

  • Update this privacy policy before implementing any changes
  • Notify existing users about the updates
  • Make such features optional (opt-in)
  • Request explicit consent before collecting any data

9. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device and we have no access to it, you have complete control:

  • You can view all your data within the app
  • You can delete your data at any time through the app settings
  • You can uninstall the app to permanently remove all data
  • You can use the app without any privacy concerns

10. Free to Use

Hydrate is completely free to use. There are no in-app purchases, subscriptions, or advertisements. We don't monetize your data because we don't collect it.

12. Summary

In simple terms: Hydrate is a privacy-first app that helps you track your daily coffee and water intake. We don't collect any data, require no registration, and everything stays on your phone. It's free, simple, and respects your privacy completely.
